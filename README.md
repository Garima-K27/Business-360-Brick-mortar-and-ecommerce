## üìà Key Insights
- Revenue is concentrated among a small set of customers and products
- High sales volumes do not always translate into high profitability due to discounting
- Several products show strong margins but limited scale, indicating growth potential
- Forecast inaccuracies increase inventory risk and working capital blockage

---

## üöÄ Growth Recommendations
- Focus on scaling high-margin, high-potential products
- Optimise pricing and reduce excessive discounting
- Improve forecast accuracy for key customers to lower inventory risk
- Use the Executive dashboard as a regular decision-making tool
